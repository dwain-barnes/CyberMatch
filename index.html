CyberMatch

How to Play

1. Click on cards to flip them over.

2. Try to find matching pairs of cybersecurity concepts.

3. You have 12 attempts to match all pairs.

4. Each correct match earns you 10 points.

5. The game ends when you match all pairs or run out of attempts.
